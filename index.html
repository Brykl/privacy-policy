Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • User name and profile information from Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp
  • User ID and messaging platform identifiers
  • Messages and content sent by users through our chatbot
  • Email address (if provided)

2. Legal basis for processing new

We process user data on the basis of legitimate interest in providing chatbot services, and/or with the user's consent where required by applicable law (including GDPR where applicable).

3. How we use information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide messaging and chatbot functionality
  • Respond to user inquiries
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure proper operation of integrations with messaging platforms

4. Platforms we work with

Our application integrates with the following platforms:

  • Facebook Messenger
  • Instagram Messaging
  • WhatsApp Business API

These platforms may provide us with user data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Use of third-party services updated

We use third-party AI services to process and respond to user messages. Specifically, we use the OpenAI API (provided by OpenAI, Inc.) for automated response generation. User messages may be securely transmitted to OpenAI for processing in accordance with OpenAI's privacy policy. We do not use this data for advertising or unrelated purposes.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. We only share data with service providers strictly necessary for the operation of the application (e.g., OpenAI API, hosting services).

7. Data storage and security new

User data is stored on secure servers located in Kazakhstan. We use PostgreSQL with appropriate access controls and take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Data retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. After that period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. User rights and data deletion updated

Users may at any time:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of their data
  • Object to or restrict processing of their data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. For Facebook and Instagram users, data deletion can also be requested via our Data Deletion Callback endpoint as required by Meta.
